Good Sandwich Recipe

Description

This recipe uses a mixture of flavors and some spices to really give you a savory treat. Trust me, you will not be disappointed...

Takes 5-10 minutes to make

Ingredients

  • Sourdough Bread
  • Honey Smoked Turkey
  • Spicy Smoked Gouda
  • Mayo
  • Mustard
  • Pickles
  • Tomatoes
  • Butter
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Toast and butter the bread
  2. Cut up some tomatoes, pickles, cheese, and anything else
  3. Put mayo on both slices of toast, where you can't see through the mayo
  4. Put a generous feeling load of mustard (if you don't do enough, you won't taste it. A good rule of thumb is to put enough that you can't see the stuff below it)
  5. Put the meat and cheese on
  6. Put the pickles and tomatoes on
  7. Add a touch of Salt and Pepper to the top
  8. Cut and enjoy!
