Chicken Soup Recipe

  • Home

Description

This chicken soup lasts multiple meals, or feeds about 8 people, and is very good for the body. It helps when you are sick or just want something easy but delicious to eat for a few days.

Takes 45-90 minutes

Ingredients

  • Whole Chicken
  • Carrots
  • Celery
  • Potatoes
  • Basil
  • Salt & Pepper
  • Olive Oil
  • Water
  • Chicken Broth

Steps

  1. Prepare the whole chicken by removing the giblets and tearing the chicken into strips
  2. Put the chicken broth and some water in the pot and let it come to a light boil
  3. Put the spices in the pot followed by the chicken and turn the stove down to a 5 or 6. Set a timer for 15 minutes
  4. Chop the vegetables up and put them in a bowl for later
  5. After about 15 minutes put the vegetables in and set a timer for 30 minutes
  6. After 30 minutes turn the stove off and don't move the pot
  7. Serve and enjoy!
